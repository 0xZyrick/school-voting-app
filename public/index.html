Programs

Word formation

A game where contestants form as many words as possible from random letters within 60 seconds.

Contestants identify scrambled English words within 60 seconds. Each correct word earns 10 points.

Contestants craft imaginative short stories based on given topics, using drama or playlet formats.

Application Process

Follow these steps to complete your application smoothly.

Step1: Registration

Schools register by submitting their contestants, with a registration fee of N5,000 per participant. Ensure all students are eligible to compete and follow the registration guidelines.

Step 2: Workshop Training

Complete the application form with all necessary information.

Step 3: Competition Stages

The competition will proceed through group stages, quarter-finals, semi-finals, and the grand finals. Contestants will be evaluated based on performance, with voting and judges' scores determining the winners.
